# Location-Based Analysis of Restaurants

## Objective

Perform a geographical analysis of restaurants using location-based attributes such as latitude, longitude, city, and locality. The goal is to identify restaurant concentration, rating trends, cuisine preferences, and pricing patterns across different locations.

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains information about restaurants, including:

* Restaurant Name
* City
* Locality
* Latitude
* Longitude
* Cuisines
* Aggregate Rating
* Price Range
* Online Delivery Availability

---

## Technologies Used

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Jupyter Notebook

---

## Analysis Performed

### 1. Restaurant Distribution Analysis

* Explored latitude and longitude coordinates.
* Visualized restaurant locations using scatter plots.
* Identified geographical concentration patterns.

### 2. City-wise Analysis

* Grouped restaurants by city.
* Calculated restaurant counts per city.
* Computed average ratings for each city.

### 3. Locality-wise Analysis

* Identified localities with the highest restaurant concentration.
* Compared restaurant density across different areas.

### 4. Price Range Analysis

* Calculated average price ranges by city.
* Examined pricing trends across locations.

### 5. Cuisine Analysis

* Determined dominant cuisines in different cities.
* Compared cuisine preferences across geographical regions.

## Key Insights

* Restaurants are concentrated in a few major metropolitan areas.
* Delhi NCR contains the highest concentration of restaurants in the dataset.
* Connaught Place emerged as one of the most restaurant-dense localities.
* Cities with larger restaurant populations do not necessarily have higher ratings.
* Higher-priced restaurants generally tend to achieve better ratings.
* Cuisine preferences vary significantly across cities and localities.
* Location plays an important role in restaurant popularity and customer ratings.

---

## Conclusion

This project analyzed the geographical distribution of restaurants using latitude and longitude data. City-wise and locality-wise analysis revealed important patterns related to restaurant concentration, ratings, cuisines, and pricing. The findings demonstrate how location influences restaurant performance and customer preferences.
